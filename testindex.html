Hello there, and welcome to Nagyvázsony,
a small village in Hungary, north-west of Lake Balaton. This page is showing what makes Nagyvázsony my personal favorite place. Enjoy scrolling and discovering.

Fekete Sereg

Monastary

For me, the heart of Nagyvázsony. It is not only the local contact point for young people, but also my host organization during my voluntary service, through which I first came into contact with this small Hungarian village near Lake Balaton.

The organization has its local youth center in the center of Nagyvázsony and shares the building of a former elementary school with other facilities such as a small café.

In this youth center I was able to get in touch with the local youth besides my fellow volunteers from all over Europe and got my introduction to the life, language, and culture of Hungary.
